FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Telecel Flex Loans.

What is Telecel Flex Loan?
+
Flex Loan is a mobile loan on Telecel Cash that allows eligible subscribers to borrow money directly into their Telecel Cash wallet — instantly, 24/7, without visiting a branch.
Who is eligible to apply?
+
You must be 18 years or older with an active Telecel Cash wallet. This service is exclusively for Telecel Cash users — regular subscribers without a Telecel Cash wallet are not eligible.
Who funds the Flex Loan?
+
The loan is funded by First Atlantic Bank (FAB), a licensed financial institution in Ghana. Telecel Ghana facilitates access through the Telecel Cash mobile platform.
How do I apply for a Flex Loan?
+
Apply through this portal by clicking any "Apply" button, reviewing your loan summary, then proceeding to fill your application form. Or use the USSD loan menu on your phone. The entire process takes under 5 minutes.
What fees are charged?
+
A service fee of 8.9% per 7-day period applies. For example, a 4-week (28-day) loan has 4 × 8.9% = 35.6% total fee on the principal. If not repaid by the due date, a one-time late payment penalty of 12.5% is added. No other hidden fees apply.
What is the maximum repayment period?
+
Loans can be repaid over a period ranging from 7 days (1 week) up to a maximum of 26 weeks / 6 months (approximately 182 days). You may repay early — partially or in full — at any time with no early repayment penalty.
How is the total repayment calculated?
+
Total Repayment = Principal + (Principal × 8.9% × Number of 7-day periods). Example: GHS 1,000 over 4 weeks: GHS 1,000 + (GHS 1,000 × 8.9% × 4) = GHS 1,000 + GHS 356 = GHS 1,356. Use our live calculator above to see any combination instantly.
Can I have more than one active loan?
+
No. You cannot access another Flex Loan while you have an existing outstanding loan. Once fully repaid, you can immediately apply for a new one with a potentially higher limit.
How do I repay the loan?
+
You can repay by: (1) allowing automatic deduction from your Telecel Cash wallet on the due date, or (2) manually repaying — partially or in full — via the repayment option in your Telecel Cash menu any time before the due date.
What happens if I don't repay on time?
+
A one-time late fee of 12.5% is added to your outstanding balance. Telecel Cash may also automatically debit your wallet for the outstanding amount whenever funds are available. Repeated late payments may reduce your future borrowing limit.
