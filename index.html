Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you apply.

Who qualifies for a Telecel Loan? +

Any active Telecel Cash subscriber with at least 3 months of account history is eligible to apply. The more regularly you use Telecel Cash for transactions — airtime, transfers, bill payments, merchant purchases — the higher your loan limit. New users can still apply and will be assessed based on available activity data.

How quickly will I receive my money? +

Approved loans are disbursed directly to your Telecel Cash wallet. Most users receive their funds within 24 hours of approval. Active and high-scoring users often receive disbursement within minutes of approval.

What is the interest rate on Telecel Loans? +

The standard monthly interest rate is 4.9%. First-time borrowers enjoy 0% interest on their very first loan. Loyal and high-activity Telecel Cash users may qualify for preferential lower rates over time. All charges are disclosed clearly before you confirm your application — no hidden fees.

How do I repay my loan? +

Repayment is made through your Telecel Cash wallet. You can repay in full before the due date (no early repayment fee) or in scheduled instalments depending on your loan type. You'll receive SMS reminders before your repayment date.

Can I increase my loan limit? +

Absolutely. Your loan limit grows automatically as you repay on time and continue using Telecel Cash actively. Each successful loan cycle can result in a higher limit on your next application. There is no manual process required — the system updates your score automatically.

What happens if I miss a repayment? +

Missing a repayment may affect your eligibility for future loans and may result in a late fee. We strongly advise contacting our support team before your due date if you anticipate difficulty repaying. Early communication helps us find a solution before any penalties apply.
