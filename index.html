Frequently Asked Questions

How long does approval take? ▼
Most applications are approved within 15 minutes. Our instant verification system checks your eligibility in real-time, so you get a decision almost immediately after submitting your application.
Who is eligible for Telecel Loans? ▼
Only Telecel subscribers are eligible for Telecel Loans. You need to be at least 18 years old, have an active Telecel account, and a stable source of income. The longer you've been using Telecel, the higher your chances to qualify for larger loan amounts.
What is the maximum loan amount? ▼
You can borrow between GHS 1,000 and GHS 50,000 depending on your Telecel history, income, and credit profile. Your maximum loan amount is determined by our risk assessment algorithm based on your Telecel usage history and financial situation.
What are the interest rates? ▼
Our interest rates are tiered based on your repayment period: 8.9% for 7 days, increasing for longer terms up to 6 months. All rates are transparent with no hidden fees. You'll see the exact amount you'll pay before confirming your application.
How do I repay my loan? ▼
Repayment is automatic and convenient. Your repayment is deducted directly from your Telecel Cash account on the due date. You can also make early payments anytime without penalties through our mobile app or website.
Is my data secure? ▼
Yes, absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and financial information. Your data is never shared with third parties without your consent, and we comply with all data protection regulations.
