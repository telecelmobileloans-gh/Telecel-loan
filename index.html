Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does approval take? ▼
Most applications are approved within 15 minutes. Our instant verification system checks your eligibility in real-time, any time of day, including weekends.
Who is eligible for Telecel Loans? ▼
Only active Telecel subscribers are eligible. You need to be at least 18 years old with a registered Telecel Cash account and a verifiable income source. The longer your Telecel history, the higher your eligible amount.
What is the maximum loan amount? ▼
You can borrow between GHS 1,000 and GHS 50,000 depending on your Telecel subscriber history, income level, and credit profile. First-time borrowers may start at a lower limit which increases with good repayment behaviour.
What are the interest rates? ▼
Our rates are tiered by repayment period: 11.5% for 7 days, stepping down progressively to 8% for 180 days. All rates are shown upfront in the calculator — no surprises at repayment time.
How do I repay my loan? ▼
Repayment is automatic — the total amount is deducted from your Telecel Cash account on the due date. You can also make early repayments any time without any penalty fee.
Is my personal data secure? ▼
Yes. We use 256-bit bank-level encryption and strict security protocols. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
What happens if I miss a repayment? ▼
Missing a repayment may affect your future borrowing limit and incur a small late fee. We recommend ensuring sufficient funds in your Telecel Cash account before the due date. Contact support if you anticipate difficulty.
