Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How quickly is my loan approved?
▼
Most applications receive an instant decision within 2–5 minutes. Our automated system verifies your Telecel subscriber status and eligibility in real time, so you know your outcome almost immediately after submitting.
Who qualifies for Telecel Loans?
▼
You must be an active Telecel subscriber aged 18 or above with a stable source of income. The longer your Telecel account history, the higher your chances of approval and the larger your potential loan amount.
What are the loan limits?
▼
Loans range from GHS 100 to GHS 50,000. Your specific limit is calculated by our risk engine based on your Telecel usage history, repayment record, and income profile.
How are interest rates calculated?
▼
Rates are annual percentage rates (p.a.) applied pro-rata to your loan period: 11.5% p.a. for 7 days, scaling down to 5.5% p.a. for 6 months. The daily rate is divided by 365 and multiplied by your number of days. You'll always see the exact repayment amount before confirming — no surprises.
How do I repay my loan?
▼
Repayment is automatically deducted from your Telecel Cash wallet on the due date. You can also repay early at any time with no penalties, directly through our app or website.
Is my personal information safe?
▼
Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption and comply with all applicable data protection laws. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties without explicit consent.
