FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Telecel Flex Loans.

What is Telecel Flex Loan?
+
Flex Loan is a mobile loan on Telecel Cash that allows eligible subscribers to borrow money directly into their Telecel Cash wallet — instantly, 24/7, without visiting a branch.
Who is eligible to apply?
+
You must be 18 years or older with an active Telecel Cash wallet. This service is exclusively for Telecel Cash users — regular subscribers without a Telecel Cash wallet are not eligible.
Who funds the Flex Loan?
+
The loan is funded by First Atlantic Bank (FAB), a licensed financial institution in Ghana. Telecel Ghana facilitates access through the Telecel Cash mobile platform.
How do I apply for a Flex Loan?
+
Apply through this portal by entering your Telecel Cash number, or via the USSD loan menu on your phone. The entire process takes under 5 minutes with no documents required.
What fees are charged?
+
A service fee of 8.9% per month applies. If not repaid by the due date, a one-time late payment penalty of 12.5% is added to the outstanding balance. No other hidden fees apply.
What is the loan term?
+
The standard loan term is 30 days from disbursement. You may repay early (partially or in full) at any time with no early repayment penalty.
What will I see before accepting the loan?
+
Before accepting, you clearly see: loan amount, term, service fee, total repayment, due date, and any applicable late fees. You must explicitly accept before any funds are disbursed.
Can I have more than one active loan?
+
No. You cannot access another Flex Loan while you have an existing outstanding loan. Once fully repaid, you can immediately apply for a new one.
How do I repay the loan?
+
You can repay by: (1) allowing automatic deduction from your Telecel Cash wallet on the due date, or (2) manually repaying — partially or in full — via the repayment option in your Telecel Cash menu any time.
What happens if I don't repay on time?
+
A one-time late fee of 12.5% is added to your outstanding balance. Telecel Cash may also automatically debit your wallet for the outstanding amount whenever funds are available.
