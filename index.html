Frequently Asked Questions

How long does approval take? ▼
Most applications are approved within 15 minutes. Our instant verification system checks your eligibility in real-time.
Who is eligible for Telecel Loans? ▼
Only Telecel subscribers are eligible. You need to be at least 18 years old with an active Telecel account and stable income. The longer you've been using Telecel, the higher your chances to qualify for larger amounts.
What is the maximum loan amount? ▼
You can borrow between GHS 1,000 and GHS 50,000 depending on your Telecel history, income, and credit profile.
What are the interest rates? ▼
Our interest rates are tiered based on repayment period: 11.5% for 7 days, decreasing for longer terms. All rates are transparent with no hidden fees.
How do I repay my loan? ▼
Repayment is automatic. Your repayment is deducted from your Telecel Cash account on the due date. You can also make early payments anytime without penalties.
Is my data secure? ▼
Yes. We use bank-level encryption and security protocols. Your data is never shared with third parties without your consent.
