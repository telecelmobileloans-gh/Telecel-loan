FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Telecel Flex Loans.

What is Telecel Flex Loan?
+
Flex Loan is a mobile loan available on Telecel Cash that allows eligible subscribers to borrow money directly into their Telecel Cash wallet — instantly, 24/7, without visiting a branch.
Who is eligible to apply?
+
You must be 18 years or older and have an active Telecel Cash wallet. This service is exclusively for Telecel Cash users — regular subscribers without a Telecel Cash wallet are not eligible.
Who funds the Flex Loan?
+
The loan is funded by First Atlantic Bank (FAB), a licensed financial institution in Ghana. Telecel Ghana facilitates access through the Telecel Cash mobile platform.
How do I apply for a Flex Loan?
+
Apply online through this portal by verifying your Telecel Cash number, or through the loan application menu on your mobile phone via the Telecel Cash USSD platform. The process takes under 5 minutes.
What fees are charged on the loan?
+
A service fee of 8.9% per month applies to all Flex Loans. If the loan is not repaid by the due date, a one-time late payment penalty of 12.5% will be added. No other hidden fees apply.
What is the loan term?
+
The standard loan term is 30 days, starting from the date the loan is credited to your Telecel Cash wallet. You may repay early (partially or in full) at any time without penalty.
What information will I see before accepting the loan?
+
Before accepting, you will clearly see: the loan amount, loan term, service fees, total repayment amount, due date, and any applicable late payment fees. You must explicitly accept before any funds are disbursed.
Can I have more than one Flex Loan at a time?
+
No. You cannot access another Flex Loan if you have an existing loan that has not been fully repaid. Once your current loan is settled, you can immediately apply for a new one.
How do I repay the loan?
+
You can repay by: (1) allowing automatic deduction from your Telecel Cash wallet on the due date, or (2) manually repaying — partially or fully — using the loan repayment option in your Telecel Cash menu at any time.
What happens if I don't repay on time?
+
If repayment is not made by the due date, a one-time late payment fee of 12.5% will be applied to your outstanding balance. Telecel Cash may also automatically debit your wallet for the outstanding amount when funds become available.
