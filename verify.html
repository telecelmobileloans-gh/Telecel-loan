Frequently Asked Questions

How long does approval take? ▼
Most applications are approved within 2-5 minutes. Our instant verification system checks your eligibility in real-time, so you get a decision almost immediately after submitting your application.
What are the eligibility requirements? ▼
To qualify for a Telecel Loan, you need to be at least 18 years old, have an active Telecel Cash account, and a stable source of income. No collateral is required. We consider your employment status, monthly income, and credit history.
What is the maximum loan amount? ▼
You can borrow between GHS 100 and GHS 50,000 depending on your income and credit profile. Your maximum loan amount is determined by our risk assessment algorithm based on your financial history and current situation.
What is the interest rate? ▼
Our interest rate is 6% per month on the outstanding balance. All rates are transparent with no hidden fees. You'll see the exact amount you'll pay before confirming your application.
How do I repay my loan? ▼
Repayment is automatic and convenient. Your monthly installments are deducted directly from your Telecel Cash account on the due date. You can also make early payments anytime without penalties through our mobile app.
Is my data secure? ▼
Yes, absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and financial information. Your data is never shared with third parties without your consent, and we comply with all data protection regulations.
